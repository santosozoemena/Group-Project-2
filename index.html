Spotify Music Insights

Overview

We are looking at the Spotify worldwide daily song ranking dataset as a reference, and using it to scrape spotify data. Consisting of 3 views, we plan on creating an interactive map of the world that will visualize our data by country and region. Second view will informatively display popularity vs population by country. View 3 will measure a song by time series and will display how long it held popularity.
